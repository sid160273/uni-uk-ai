General summary [from website]
Through our teaching and research, our mission is to inspire our students to be the responsible business leaders of tomorrow, giving them the knowledge, skills and foresight to embrace industry challenges and lead in developing successful, sustainable national and international business operations. 
We are divided into four Schools:
The School of Accounting, Finance and Law, where our focus is on digital technologies, professionalism and opportunity, ensuring you develop the skills and experience to compete at the highest levels of business, finance and law.
The School of Aviation, Marketing and Tourism, which takes a global, multicultural approach: embedding sustainability into our practice-based teaching and preparing you for success whether your specialism is business, marketing, aviation, tourism or hospitality.
The School of Leadership and Management uses supportive teaching and innovative practice to help you develop the essential business expertise and management skills you need to be a successful, flexible and forward-thinking business leader.
Graduate School of Business works in close collaboration with local, national and international business communities to deliver postgraduate courses that prepare you to thrive in the ever-evolving world of work, whatever your specialist area of research.
